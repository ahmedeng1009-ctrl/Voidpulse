## STATS SOURCES
- **Hall et al. (2019)** — NIH randomized controlled trial: ultra-processed diet caused participants to consume ~500 kcal/day more (*Cell Metabolism*, Vol. 30)
- **Monteiro et al. / NOVA Classification** — Ultra-processed foods comprise ~57–60% of caloric intake in the U.S. (*BMJ Open*, 2016)
- **Vighi et al. (2008)** — "The gut is the largest immune organ in the body, housing ~70% of immune cells" (*Clinical & Experimental Immunology*)
- **Leaky Gut / Intestinal Permeability** — Chassaing et al. research on emulsifiers disrupting gut mucosa (*Nature*, 2015)
