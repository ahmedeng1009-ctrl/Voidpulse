**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]**
> *[Single white text on black screen. Dead silence for 0.5s. Then a low, ominous drone hits.]*

"The Fed just handed billionaires **$4 trillion**. While you slept."

---

**[BUILD — 0:03–0:20]**
> *[Cut to slow-motion footage of the Federal Reserve building. Red data overlays flash across the screen. Heart rate monitor sound effect pulses beneath the music.]*

"Every time the Fed cuts interest rates, asset prices surge."

"Stocks. Real estate. Private equity. Things **you don't own.**"

"The top **1%** holds **32%** of all U.S. stock market wealth."

"The bottom **50%**? Less than **1%.**"

"So when the Fed moves — it moves *for them.*"

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:38]**
> *[Hard cut to black. Then slow zoom on a clock ticking at 3AM. Text overlays read: "YOU WERE ASLEEP." Strings swell beneath the drone.]*

"This isn't an accident. It's called the **Wealth Effect.**"

"The Fed inflates asset prices *on purpose* — so the rich *feel* richer and spend more."

"That spending is supposed to trickle down to you."

"It has been **40 years**. Still waiting?"

"While you earned **3% in a savings account** — they earned **26% in the market.** Same year."

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:38–0:50]**
> *[Screen goes completely black. One line of white text fades in slowly: "The system is working exactly as designed." Voiceover drops to near-whisper. Music cuts to silence on the last word.]*

"The system isn't broken. It was built this way — and *you* were never in the blueprint."

"**Has your bank account grown since the last Fed cut?**"
