# How Your Phone Is Making You Dumber Every Day | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a glowing phone screen reflecting in blank, unblinking eyes. Dead silence for one beat before a deep bass drop.]*

"Your phone isn't just distracting you. It is **rewiring your brain.**"

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Rapid-cut visuals of brain scans, decaying neural pathways rendered in dark red, timestamps ticking upward on a black screen. Numbers flash like a doomsday counter.]*

"The average human attention span has collapsed from **12 seconds in 2000 to just 8 seconds today.** Less than a goldfish. Scientists have a name for what's replacing your ability to think deeply — they call it **cognitive offloading.** You are literally outsourcing your memory, your judgment, and your reasoning... to a device that profits from keeping you empty."

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Single harsh white light. Slow zoom on a cracked phone screen. Text burns onto the screen: "YOU ARE THE PRODUCT."]*

"A 2021 study from University of Texas found that simply having your phone **face-down on a desk** — powered off — still measurably reduces your available brainpower. It doesn't need to be on. It doesn't need to notify you. Its **mere presence** is enough to drain your cognitive capacity. The machine has already won the room before you even unlock it."

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Screen goes completely black. A single notification ping echoes like a drip in an empty cave. One line of white text fades in slowly: "You felt that, didn't you?"]*

"Every app on your phone was engineered by teams of psychologists, neuroscientists, and behavioral designers — one single goal: **make you come back.** Not for your benefit. For theirs."

"**You were never the user. You were always the product.**"
