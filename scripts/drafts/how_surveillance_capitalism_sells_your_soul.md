# How Surveillance Capitalism Sells Your Soul | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a human eye reflecting a phone screen. Screen flickers. Heartbeat sound drops to silence.]*

"You are not the customer. You are the product. And you've already been sold."

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Rapid data stream visuals — location pins, search queries, heartbeat patterns cascading across a black screen. Numbers multiply faster than the eye can read.]*

"Every scroll. Every pause. Every search at **3AM** when you thought no one was watching. Recorded. Packaged. Auctioned. The global surveillance economy is now worth **over $422 billion dollars** — built entirely from the raw material of your behaviour. Your fears. Your desires. Your weaknesses."

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Single sentence appears on screen in blood-red text: 'They know you better than you know yourself.' Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the viewer's reflection trapped inside a phone screen.]*

"Facebook's own internal research confirmed their algorithm can detect **depression before you feel it**. Insurance companies are quietly purchasing your personal data to predict — and deny — your future. You didn't sign away your privacy. You traded it for a dopamine hit that was **engineered to addict you.**"

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Screen goes completely black. A single white cursor blinks. Then the words: 'THEY ALREADY KNOW YOU'RE WATCHING THIS.' fade in slowly.]*

"The most terrifying part? Knowing this changes nothing. Because the system was never designed to be escaped — only monetized."

"**You are the data. You are the product. You are the profit.**"
