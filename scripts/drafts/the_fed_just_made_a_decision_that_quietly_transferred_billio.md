# The Fed's Silent Heist | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:03]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a savings account balance slowly draining. Red digits. Dead silence, then a single low drone hits.]*

"Your savings lost **$900** this year. The Fed decided that."

---

**[BUILD — 0:03–0:20]**
> *[Cut to black-and-white footage of the Federal Reserve building. Slow zoom. Timestamp overlays flicker like a surveillance feed.]*

"In 2022, the Fed held interest rates near **zero** — for two full years — while inflation hit **9.1%.**"

"That gap — between what your bank paid you and what inflation stole — is called the **real return.**"

"For most Americans, that number was **negative 7%.**"

"You didn't lose money in a crash. You lost it sitting still — in an account you thought was safe."

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:38]**
> *[Hard cut. Glitching red text overlays. Stock market charts spike violently upward as the voiceover drops to nearly a whisper.]*

"While your savings were being quietly eroded —"

"The S&P 500 handed the **top 1%** — who own **89% of all stocks** — a combined wealth gain of **$6.5 trillion.**"

"The same low rates that punished your savings account — **funded their portfolios.**"

"This wasn't an accident. Low rates were the policy. Your loss — was the mechanism."

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:38–0:50]**
> *[Screen goes completely black. One line of white text fades in: "BY DESIGN." The drone music cuts to silence.]*

"The greatest wealth transfers in history don't happen in crashes — they happen in the quiet."

"**Did your bank ever warn you? YES or NO.**"
