# The Secret Algorithm That Controls What You Think | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a human eye. A faint grid of code reflects across the iris. Screen flickers to black.]*

"You haven't had an original thought today. It gave you all of them."

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Cut to cascading data streams. Red timestamps flash. A silhouette sits alone, bathed in screen light.]*

"The average person spends **2 hours and 27 minutes** on social media daily. In that window, a machine makes **over 10,000 micro-decisions** about what you see — what angers you, what seduces you, what you believe. Facebook's own internal research confirmed it could shift the **emotional states of 689,000 users** without a single person consenting."

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Static cut. The silhouette turns — it has no face. Numbers bleed across a world map. Courtroom documents flash subliminally.]*

"This isn't theory. A 2023 Senate hearing revealed these systems are deliberately engineered to maximize outrage — because outrage keeps you watching. You're not the user. You are the **product being processed.** Your opinions, your fears, your identity — optimized. Packaged. Sold. Every second you stay on screen, the model gets smarter. You do not."

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Screen goes completely dark. A single cursor blinks. Then one line of text appears: "WHO TAUGHT YOU TO THINK THIS?"]*

"The most dangerous prison ever built has no walls, no guards, and no locks. It only needs your attention."

"**And you gave it willingly.**"
