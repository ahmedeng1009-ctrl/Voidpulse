# The Dark History of How Social Media Hijacks Your Brain | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of an eye reflecting a phone screen. Pulse sound effect. Screen flickers to black.]*

"What if the most addictive drug ever created… fits in your pocket?"

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Rapid cuts of notification badges multiplying. Red numbers cascade across the screen. Heartbeat sound syncs with each ping.]*

"In 2004, dopamine researchers warned that variable reward systems — the same mechanism behind slot machines — were being quietly engineered into social feeds. By 2023, the average human touches their phone **2,617 times per day.** Former engineers at Facebook called it by one name internally: **The Slot Machine.**"

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Screen splits. One side shows a Vegas casino floor. The other side shows someone scrolling in a dark room. They are identical in motion.]*

"Tristan Harris, a former Google design ethicist, revealed that teams of **PhD-level psychologists** were hired — not to protect you — but to calculate the exact moment your willpower collapses. Every infinite scroll. Every autoplay. Every red notification dot. Engineered. Deliberately. Against you."

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Camera slowly pulls back from the eye. The phone screen is the only light source in total darkness. The light shrinks. Then vanishes.]*

"The system was never broken. It was built this way — and it has been refining itself on your behavior for twenty years."

"**You are not the user. You are the product being consumed.**"
