# Billionaires Make More While You Sleep | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a sleeping face. Clock ticking. Dollar counter spinning in the corner. Dead silence — then a low bass drop.]*

"Elon Musk made **$22 million** while you slept last night."

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Cut to black screen. White numbers rapidly multiplying. Each number change synced to a heartbeat sound.]*

"That's not a yearly salary. That's not a bonus. That's **$1.5 million every single hour** — while he dreamed. While you worked. While you saved every receipt. The average American will earn that amount in **422 years** of labor. You will never close that gap. It is mathematically impossible."

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Screen fractures like broken glass. A single dim light. Shadow of a person hunched over a desk.]*

"And here's what they don't tell you. The system wasn't broken by billionaires. It was **built for them.** Their wealth doesn't come from working harder. It compounds in silence — in stocks, in assets, in structures designed by the same governments that told you to **just save more.**"

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Slow zoom into a black void. A single white line of text fades in: "The game was never yours to win." Heartbeat flatlines.]*

"You woke up today and lost ground you didn't even know you were standing on."

"**They don't need your hours. They own the clock.**"
