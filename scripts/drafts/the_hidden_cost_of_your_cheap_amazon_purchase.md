# The Hidden Cost of Your Cheap Amazon Purchase | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a hand clicking "Buy Now." Screen glows cold blue in the dark. Hard cut to black.]*

"That $8 purchase didn't cost you $8."

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Fast-cut montage: warehouse workers running, package conveyor belts, a clock ticking. Red statistics burn onto the screen one by one.]*

"Amazon warehouse workers walk **up to 15 miles per shift** — in facilities where the injury rate is **twice the industry average.** Over **1,000 workers are injured every single week.** And the median Amazon warehouse salary keeps them **below the poverty line** in most major cities."

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Slow zoom into a cardboard box dissolving into satellite footage of a deforested Amazon basin. Ominous drone sound builds.]*

"Amazon generated **over 700 million pounds of plastic packaging waste** in a single year. Most of it ends up in oceans and landfills — because fewer than **14% of flexible plastics can actually be recycled.** The planet absorbs what your doorstep doesn't."

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Screen goes completely black. A single white tracking number fades in, then dissolves. Silence for one beat. Then the voiceover hits low and cold.]*

"Every algorithm knows exactly what you'll buy next. Every shortcut has a price. You just never see the invoice."

"**Someone always pays. It just isn't you.**"

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

| Element | Details |
|---|---|
| **Voiceover pace** | Slow, cold, and deliberate — pause after every statistic |
| **Music** | Dark ambient industrial drone, no melody, tension builds to silence at outro |
| **Font style** | White sans-serif, all caps, stark against black — statistics appear as sudden hard cuts |
| **Color grade** | Desaturated cold blue throughout, single red accent on injury statistics |
| **Sound design** | Conveyor belt sounds, muffled footsteps, a single heartbeat before the final line |
| **Pacing** | Quick cuts during BUILD, slow creeping zoom during TWIST, full stop for OUTRO |
