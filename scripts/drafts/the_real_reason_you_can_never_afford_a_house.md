# The Real Reason You Can Never Afford a House | YouTube Short Script
**Niche:** Scary Real Statistics Visualized
**Duration:** ~50 seconds
**Style:** Dramatic / Conspiracy-core

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**
> *[Extreme close-up of a "SOLD" sign. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal an endless street of identical houses. Deep bass drone begins.]*

"You were never meant to own one."

---

**[BUILD — 0:05–0:20]**
> *[Black screen. Red numbers cascade downward like digital rain. Each stat slams onto screen with a shudder cut.]*

"In 2024, **institutional investors own over 40% of single-family rental homes** in some U.S. markets. BlackRock. Vanguard. Invitation Homes. They didn't buy neighborhoods. **They bought your future.** The median home price has risen **over 400% since 1994** — while wages grew less than **160%.**"

---

**[TWIST — 0:20–0:35]**
> *[Slow zoom into a corporate glass tower reflected in a flooded suburban street. Distorted choir swells underneath.]*

"Here's what they don't say out loud. When a corporation buys 1,000 homes before sunrise — **you were never competing in the same market.** You were the product. Your rent. Your desperation. Your lease renewal. **Packaged. Traded. Profited from.**"

---

**[OUTRO / CTA — 0:35–0:50]**
> *[Single candle flame in a dark room. Slow fade to black. One final low frequency pulse hits on the last word.]*

"They call it the housing market. But a market implies you had a chance to buy."

"**You were always just inventory.**"
